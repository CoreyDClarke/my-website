Artist Statement

From a young age my family has regarded me as a stongly tactile person. Experiencing reality to a certain degree through my hands. It is from this body part from which most of my art has orignated. For this reason I was particularly drawn towards the world of art because I could create something I experinced using my hands and eysight. When I create art I strive and sometimes struggle to create a piece that is inspired by the balence and aesthetic quality between simplicty and complexity. When I find these represented in reality and see a dreamlike, perhaps maybe bold or flashy, indescribable quality I am often drawn towards it. Through art I often try to recreate this, and the feelings of mystery, wonder, joy or deep emotion that I feel as a result.

The kind of art media(s) that I particularly specialize in are probably:

  • Drawing
  • Acrylic Painting
  • Creative Writing
  • Photography

I have also practiced/worked in the following medias:

  • Ceramics
  • Sculpture
  • Printmaking
  • Bookarts
  • Glass fusing
  • Watercolor
  • Etc.
